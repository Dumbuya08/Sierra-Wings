Our Mission

To provide immediate, reliable medical supply delivery to every corner of Sierra Leone, ensuring that no patient goes without life saving treatment due to transportation barriers.

SierraWings bridges the gap between medical facilities and patients in remote areas, using autonomous drone technology to deliver medications, medical supplies, and emergency treatments within minutes rather than hours.

Core Values

  • Accessibility for all patients
  • Speed in emergency situations
  • Reliability in every delivery
  • Safety first approach
