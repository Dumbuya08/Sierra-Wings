Sierra Leone Data Protection Authority

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you can file a complaint with the Sierra Leone Data Protection Authority.

Contact Information:

Email: complaints@dataprotection.sl
Phone: +232 22 227 610
Address: Tower Hill, Freetown, Sierra Leone

Required Information:
  • Your full name and contact details
  • Description of the data protection concern
  • Evidence or documentation
  • Steps taken to resolve the issue
