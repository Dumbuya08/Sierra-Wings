Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2025

Last Updated: July 14, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our Service:

Personal Information
  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Physical address and location data
  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Payment and billing information
Medical Information
  • Medical delivery requests and prescriptions
  • Emergency contact information
  • Allergy and medical condition data (patient accounts)
  • Healthcare provider credentials and licenses
Technical Information
  • Device information and IP addresses
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Usage data and interaction patterns
  • GPS coordinates and location tracking

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Processing and fulfilling medical delivery requests
  • Providing real time tracking and updates
  • Communicating with users about their deliveries
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Ensuring safety and regulatory compliance
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized access
  • Legal compliance and emergency response

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

Healthcare Providers

Medical information is shared with registered healthcare providers to process delivery requests and ensure appropriate medical care.

Service Providers

Third party vendors who assist with platform operations, including payment processing, data storage, and communication services.

Legal Requirements

When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of SierraWings, our users, or others.

Emergency Situations

In medical emergencies, we may share necessary information with emergency services and healthcare providers.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission and storage
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Staff training on privacy and security protocols
  • Incident response procedures for data breaches

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and support
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Improve our services and develop new features

Medical records are retained according to healthcare industry standards and regulatory requirements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non essential communications
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • File complaints with relevant regulatory authorities

7. Location Data

Our service requires location data to:

  • Process delivery requests accurately
  • Provide real time tracking updates
  • Ensure drone safety and navigation
  • Comply with aviation regulations

Location data is collected only when necessary for service delivery and is protected with appropriate security measures.

8. Third Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third party services for enhanced functionality. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us through our support channels available on the platform.
